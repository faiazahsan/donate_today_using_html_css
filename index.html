Food for a family

Food for a family takes action against food insecurity. With the community's help, we cook and distribute hot meals for low-income families, seniors and others who can't cook for themselves.

Shelter for a family

We take a family-centred, holistic approach and ensure that people with particular vulnerabilities are supported. We coordinate services and participate in planning with community partners.

Education for a child

We are working to better identify and support children who are the hardest to reach. We are collaborating with governments, partners, country/regional offices, donors and communities
